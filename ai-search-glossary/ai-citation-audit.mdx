*Methodology* · *Citation & Visibility Measurement*

## Definition

An AI citation audit is a systematic evaluation of how a brand is currently represented across AI search platforms — what is being said about it, which sources are being cited, where inaccuracies or gaps exist, and how its citation footprint compares to competitors. It is the diagnostic foundation of any AI SEO engagement.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

You cannot optimize a brand's AI representation without first measuring it. An AI citation audit answers the questions that precede strategy: Is the brand being cited at all? For which queries? With what characterization? From which sources? Where are the inaccuracies? What is the competitor citation landscape? Without this baseline, AI SEO work is directionally blind. The audit converts abstract optimization goals into a specific, prioritized list of what to fix first.
