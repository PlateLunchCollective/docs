*Methodology* · *Citation & Visibility Measurement*

## Definition

A context map is PLC's proprietary diagnostic that audits how AI systems currently represent a brand — what they say about it, what sources they draw from, what topics they associate it with, and where the gaps and inaccuracies are.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

You cannot optimize what you have not measured. Most brands have no idea what ChatGPT or Perplexity says about them when asked. A context map answers that question systematically, across platforms, and produces a prioritized list of what to fix first — whether that is structured data, entity signals, third-party citations, or content gaps.
