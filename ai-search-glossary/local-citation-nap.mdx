## Definition

A local citation is any online mention of a business's Name, Address, and Phone number (NAP) — appearing in directories, review sites, news articles, social profiles, and any other web source. Local citations are the foundational building blocks of local entity presence.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

Local citations are how AI systems build confidence in a business's geographic identity. Each consistent citation is a corroborating data point that the business exists, is located where it claims, and can be reached as stated. Inconsistent citations — different address formats, disconnected phone numbers, name variations — create conflicting entity signals that undermine AI representation accuracy. For Hawaii businesses, ensuring local citation consistency across Google Business Profile, Yelp, TripAdvisor, local chambers, and Hawaii-specific directories is foundational entity hygiene.
