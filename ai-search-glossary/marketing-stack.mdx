## Definition

A marketing stack is the collection of software tools and platforms a marketing team uses to plan, execute, measure, and optimize its activities — typically including CRM, email marketing, advertising platforms, analytics, content management, and increasingly, AI tools.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

The marketing stack increasingly includes AI search monitoring and optimization tools. Brands building AI search capability need to add AI citation monitoring tools, prompt testing frameworks, and entity management systems to their stack. A fractional CMO auditing a client's marketing stack for AI search readiness identifies which tools are already in place, which need to be added, and how AI search data should flow into existing reporting and decision-making systems.
