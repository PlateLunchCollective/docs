*Core concept* · *Citation & Visibility Measurement*

## Definition

A citation signal is any web-based reference — linked or unlinked — that AI systems interpret as evidence of a brand's authority or relevance on a topic. Citation signals accumulate across independent sources and contribute to the corroboration patterns AI systems use to build entity knowledge.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

Citation signals are the currency of AI search authority. Every time an independent source references a brand in context — a review, a press mention, a directory listing, a forum post — it adds a citation signal to the brand's entity record. The aggregate of these signals, weighted by source authority and independence, determines how confidently AI systems cite the brand for relevant queries.
