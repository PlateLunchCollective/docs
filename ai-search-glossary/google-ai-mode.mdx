*Platform* · *AI Search*

## Definition

Google AI Mode is Google's conversational AI search interface that generates synthesized, multi-turn answers rather than a traditional ranked list of blue links. Distinct from AI Overviews, AI Mode is a dedicated search experience powered by Gemini.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

AI Mode represents Google's most aggressive shift toward generative search. In AI Mode, the traditional ten blue links are replaced by a synthesized answer with citations. Brands that earn citation in AI Mode appear at the highest-visibility position in Google's evolving search interface. The optimization requirements overlap significantly with AI Overviews but with greater emphasis on conversational, multi-step query handling.
