*Core concept* · *Entity & Knowledge Graph*

## Definition

A brand entity is the structured representation of a brand as a distinct, identifiable object within a knowledge graph — linked to attributes such as location, founders, products, founding date, and industry category. A brand entity is distinct from a brand's web presence: it is the abstract identity that AI systems reference across all mentions of the brand.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

The brand entity is the AI system's internal model of what a brand is. When an AI system generates a response about a brand, it draws from the brand entity — its stored attributes, relationships, and associations. A brand with a rich, accurate, well-verified entity is characterized correctly. A brand with a sparse, incomplete, or conflicting entity is characterized poorly, vaguely, or not at all. Building the brand entity is the foundational AI SEO task.
