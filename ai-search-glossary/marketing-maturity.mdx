## Definition

Marketing maturity is the degree to which a company's marketing function operates strategically, systematically, and measurably — from early-stage ad hoc activity through structured program management to fully integrated, data-driven marketing operations.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

AI search optimization is most effective at mid-to-high marketing maturity levels. Early-stage companies without stable messaging, consistent digital infrastructure, or content production capacity struggle to execute the sustained entity, content, and technical work AI SEO requires. A fractional CMO assessing marketing maturity before prescribing AI search strategy helps clients invest in the right foundations rather than jumping to citation optimization before the underlying infrastructure is in place.
