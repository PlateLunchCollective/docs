## Definition

AI agent discoverability is the degree to which a brand's content, entity signals, and digital infrastructure are accessible and legible to AI agents — autonomous systems that conduct research, make recommendations, and take actions on behalf of users — as distinct from human-facing discoverability or single-turn AI search discoverability.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

AI agents have different retrieval requirements than conversational AI search. They may need to access APIs, parse structured data, follow sameAs links, and retrieve content from multiple sources programmatically. Brands that structure their digital presence for agent-accessible retrieval — with open APIs, structured data, clear entity graphs, and machine-readable content — will capture agentic discovery that brands optimized only for human readability will miss.
