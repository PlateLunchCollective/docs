*Core concept* · *Entity & Knowledge Graph*

## Definition

A Knowledge Panel is an information box displayed on the right side of Google search results — and increasingly integrated into AI-generated answers — showing structured facts about an entity: name, description, founding date, location, social profiles, and related entities.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

A Knowledge Panel is the most visible signal that Google has successfully resolved a brand's entity. It means the Knowledge Graph has enough structured, cross-referenced data to present the brand as a known, verifiable entity rather than a pattern of keywords. Earning a Knowledge Panel is not a goal in itself — it is a byproduct of building proper entity infrastructure. When it appears, it confirms the underlying work is functioning.
