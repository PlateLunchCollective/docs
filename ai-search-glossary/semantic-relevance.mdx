## Definition

Semantic relevance is the degree to which content is contextually and conceptually related to a query or topic — assessed not by keyword matching but by meaning, entity associations, and topical relationships.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

AI systems do not rank pages by counting how many times a keyword appears. They assess meaning. A page that thoroughly covers a topic — using related concepts, named entities, and contextual language — scores higher on semantic relevance than a page that repeats a target keyword without exploring its surrounding conceptual territory. Building semantic relevance means writing like a subject matter expert, not like someone targeting a search query.
