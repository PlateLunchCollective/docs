*Core concept* · *Local & Hawaii*

## Definition

A local knowledge panel is a Knowledge Panel specifically generated for a local business — displaying the business's name, address, hours, phone number, reviews, photos, and related entities in Google's right-side panel and AI-generated local responses.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

A local knowledge panel is the most visible confirmation that Google has successfully resolved a local business's entity. It draws from Google Business Profile, local citations, schema markup, and third-party review data — and its accuracy directly reflects the health of the business's local entity signals. For Hawaii businesses, a fully populated and accurate local knowledge panel is both a customer experience asset and an AI retrieval prerequisite for local recommendation queries.
