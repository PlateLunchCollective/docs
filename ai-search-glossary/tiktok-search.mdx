*Platform* · *Social Search*

## Definition

TikTok's in-app search functionality has become a significant discovery surface — particularly among younger demographics — for product, brand, how-to, and local queries. Users submit queries directly in TikTok's search bar and receive results from TikTok's video index, filtered by relevance and creator authority.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

TikTok Search is one of the highest-growth social search surfaces and a significant AI retrieval source. AI systems that index TikTok content draw from the same pool of videos that TikTok Search surfaces — meaning TikTok SEO optimization and AI social search optimization are largely the same workstream. For brands targeting younger demographics, TikTok Search presence is a strategic priority for both social discovery and AI citation in social-sourced responses.
