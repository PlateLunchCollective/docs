*Methodology* · *Entity & Knowledge Graph*

## Definition

A consolidated entity profile is a complete, consistent, and cross-referenced set of structured data about an entity — integrating information from the brand's own website, schema markup, Wikidata, Google Business Profile, social profiles, and third-party sources into a coherent whole.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

AI systems construct their understanding of an entity from many sources. A consolidated entity profile ensures that all those sources tell the same story — consistent name, description, attributes, and relationships across every platform where the entity appears. Fragmented or inconsistent entity profiles produce fragmented AI representations. Building a consolidated profile is the entity SEO equivalent of a brand audit: it finds and fixes the gaps before AI systems do.
