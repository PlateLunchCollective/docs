## Definition

Integrated marketing is an approach that aligns all marketing channels — paid, earned, owned, and shared — around a consistent message, brand voice, and strategic objective. It ensures that every touchpoint reinforces the same positioning rather than operating in silos.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

Integrated marketing has a direct entity signal benefit: consistent messaging across owned content, press coverage, social profiles, and partner channels produces consistent co-occurrence signals that strengthen AI brand representation. A brand whose messaging fragments across channels — different value propositions on its website, its LinkedIn, and its PR — produces fragmented AI characterizations. Integration is both a marketing efficiency principle and an entity consistency strategy.
