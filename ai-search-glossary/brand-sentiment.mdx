## Definition

Brand sentiment is the qualitative tone — positive, neutral, or negative — of mentions of a brand across web content and AI-generated responses. AI systems may incorporate sentiment signals when characterizing a brand, particularly when synthesizing review content or community discussions.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

Brand sentiment affects how AI systems describe a brand's reputation and customer experience. A brand with predominantly positive community-generated content and reviews will be characterized more favorably in AI-generated summaries than a brand with mixed or negative sentiment signals. Monitoring brand sentiment in AI responses — not just mention frequency — is part of a complete AI brand management program.
