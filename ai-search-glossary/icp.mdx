## Definition

An ideal customer profile (ICP) is a detailed description of the type of company or individual most likely to derive maximum value from a product or service — and therefore most likely to become a long-term, high-value customer. ICP is used to focus marketing, sales, and product decisions.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

ICP definition informs which AI search surfaces and query types matter most for a given brand. Understanding the audience's information-seeking behavior helps translate abstract positioning into specific content and citation priorities — focusing optimization efforts on the queries, platforms, and contexts most relevant to the brand's most valuable customers.
