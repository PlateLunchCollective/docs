## Definition

Multi-platform presence is the deliberate distribution of a brand's entity signals, content, and structured data across multiple digital platforms — website, social profiles, directories, knowledge bases, and third-party publications — to build the corroborated footprint AI systems use to establish entity confidence.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

AI systems triangulate entity identity from multiple sources. A brand present only on its own website gives AI systems one data point. A brand with consistent, accurate signals on its website, LinkedIn, Wikidata, Crunchbase, Google Business Profile, and relevant industry directories gives AI systems a network of corroborating evidence. Multi-platform presence is the infrastructure layer of entity SEO.
