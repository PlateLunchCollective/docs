## Definition

No-click search is a search session in which the user's information need is satisfied directly on the SERP or by an AI assistant — without the user clicking through to any external website. It is the broader category that encompasses zero-click search, AI-generated answers, featured snippets, and knowledge panels.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

No-click search defines the fundamental tension of modern search optimization: brands need AI citation presence to remain discoverable, but AI-generated answers reduce the click-through traffic that attribution systems can track. Managing no-click search strategically means investing in AI citation for brand awareness and authority while also building content depth and follow-up intent pathways that generate clicks for high-intent queries.
