*Core concept* · *Entity & Knowledge Graph*

## Definition

Entity prominence is the relative importance of an entity within its category — how well-known, widely-referenced, and structurally significant it is compared to other entities of the same type. Prominence is a factor in both Google's Knowledge Graph construction and in AI citation weighting.

## Why It Matters for AI Search

Prominence explains why AI systems default to citing the largest, most widely-referenced players in any category. A small agency competing for AI citations in a category dominated by large enterprise players faces a prominence disadvantage that content optimization alone cannot overcome. Building prominence requires accumulating the signals of genuine importance: third-party coverage, industry citations, event participation, and the kind of on-the-record presence that makes an entity hard to ignore.
