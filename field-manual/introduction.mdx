# The PLC Field Manual

Search changed. Most agencies haven't caught up.

When someone asks ChatGPT which skincare brands are worth buying, or asks Perplexity for the best luxury hotels in a specific market, or asks Claude to recommend a SaaS tool — your website's Google ranking has nothing to do with whether you show up. The systems retrieving those answers work differently. They pull from structured knowledge, cited sources, and entities they've learned to trust.

That's the gap this manual addresses.

Plate Lunch Collective was built specifically for this moment. We've been doing SEO since 2004. We've watched every major retrieval shift — PageRank, Panda, Hummingbird, SGE, and now the full pivot to AI-native search. Each time, the underlying principle held: systems reward content that clearly answers questions and earns citations from sources they trust.

The tools changed. The principle didn't.

## What You'll Find Here

This manual covers three things:

**The Field Manual FAQ** answers the questions we get most often from clients — what AI SEO actually is, how it differs from traditional SEO, what GEO and AEO mean in practice, and what a realistic 90-day engagement looks like.

**The 90-Day Sprint Methodology** explains how we structure our work. Not as a sales document — as an honest account of what happens, in what order, and why.

**The Glossary** defines every term we use. GEO, AEO, RAG, citation architecture, retrieval layer, entity optimization — each gets its own page with a plain-language definition and the context you need to use it correctly.

## Who This Is For

Founders, marketing directors, and in-house teams trying to understand why their brand isn't showing up in AI-generated answers — and what to do about it.

If you're evaluating whether to work with us, start with [How It Works](/field-manual/how-it-works) and [The 90-Day Sprint Overview](/methodology/overview).

If you're already in a sprint and need a reference, the Glossary is your fastest path to the term you're looking for.
