# PLC Services

Plate Lunch Collective runs eight services across two tracks.

**Execution services** build the technical infrastructure that makes a brand retrievable across AI search, traditional search, and social discovery. **Strategic counsel** sits above execution — for businesses that need senior marketing judgment, not just deliverables.

## Execution Services

- [AI SEO / GEO](/services/ai-seo) — Optimize for retrieval across AI-mediated search surfaces
- [Answer Engine Optimization](/services/aeo) — Structure content to earn direct answers in AI responses
- [Entity SEO](/services/entity-seo) — Build the structured signals AI systems use to identify and trust your brand
- [Social Search Optimization](/services/social-search) — Optimize for discovery across social platforms as search surfaces
- [Citation-Ready Content](/services/citation-content) — Write and structure content that AI systems can extract and cite
- [Context Map](/services/context-map) — Map the semantic territory your brand needs to own

## Strategic Counsel

- [AI Search Visibility Assessment](/services/ai-fluency) — Find out what AI systems currently know about your brand, where the picture is wrong, and what it would take to change it
- [Fractional CMO](/services/fractional-cmo) — Senior marketing leadership on a sprint model
