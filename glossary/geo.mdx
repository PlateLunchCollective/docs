# GEO — Generative Engine Optimization

**Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** is the practice of building the entity authority and citation infrastructure that causes AI platforms to retrieve, recommend, and accurately represent your brand in generated responses.

Where [AEO](/glossary/aeo) optimizes content structure for answer extraction, GEO optimizes brand presence for brand retrieval. The goal is for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, and similar platforms to recognize your brand as a credible, relevant entity — and to surface it when your category, use case, or market comes up in a query.

## What GEO Addresses

AI language models develop associations between entities through the content they've been trained on and the sources they retrieve at query time. GEO works to strengthen those associations by:

**Citation building** — earning mentions, links, and references from sources that AI systems weight heavily: industry publications, authoritative directories, forums where your audience actually talks, and social platforms that AI systems index.

**Entity consolidation** — ensuring your brand information is consistent, complete, and unambiguous across every surface where AI systems might encounter it. Inconsistent NAP data, conflicting descriptions, and sparse entity records all reduce retrieval accuracy.

**Topical authority** — publishing enough depth on the topics your brand should own that AI systems associate your brand with those topics, not just your brand name.

**Social search presence** — Reddit threads, LinkedIn posts, YouTube content, and similar surfaces are active citation inputs for many AI platforms. GEO includes these as retrieval surfaces, not just distribution channels.

## GEO vs. Traditional SEO

Traditional SEO targets Google's ranking algorithm. GEO targets the training data and retrieval logic of generative AI systems. The channels overlap partially — a strong backlink profile helps both — but GEO requires citation sources and content types that traditional SEO has never prioritized.

## GEO vs. AEO

[AEO](/glossary/aeo) is about content extraction. GEO is about brand authority. AEO determines whether AI systems can accurately pull answers from your content. GEO determines whether AI systems think of your brand at all when a relevant question comes up.

Both are components of [AI SEO](/glossary/ai-seo). Neither works in isolation.
