# AI Brand Score

**AI brand score** is a measure of how consistently and accurately AI platforms retrieve, represent, and recommend your brand across a defined set of queries. It's a diagnostic framework, not a single metric — no platform produces an official AI brand score the way Google produces a PageRank.

The concept exists because brands needed a way to assess their AI retrieval visibility before the tooling caught up. Running structured query tests across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini and evaluating the results gives you a working picture of where your brand stands in AI retrieval.

## What It Measures

A useful AI brand score evaluation looks at several dimensions:

**Retrieval rate** — across a set of category and use-case queries relevant to your brand, how often does your brand appear in the generated response at all?

**Accuracy** — when your brand is mentioned, is the description accurate? Are the products, services, markets, and differentiators represented correctly?

**Sentiment** — is the brand presented neutrally, positively, or negatively?

**Citation frequency** — is your brand being cited as a source, or only mentioned in passing?

**Platform coverage** — does retrieval performance vary significantly across platforms? A brand that appears in Perplexity but not ChatGPT has a platform-specific gap worth investigating.

## How to Run a Basic Audit

Pick 15-20 queries that a potential customer might ask when looking for what you offer. Run each query across at least three AI platforms. Record whether your brand appears, what's said about it, and which competitors appear instead.

The pattern that emerges is your baseline. Subsequent sprints track movement against that baseline.
